Case Study 1

Digital transformation for a 50-year community centre

Full digital transformation of Healesville Living & Learning Centre — connecting locals to services, programs, and each other.

Community Sector Neighbourhood House Victoria

Services: Website rebuild · Social Planet CRM · Social media strategy · Brand guidelines · Analytics

🏛️ Healesville Living & Learning Centre Add project screenshot here
The Challenge

An outdated digital presence failing the community

The existing website was outdated with broken links and poor mobile experience. There was no CRM integration, brand inconsistency across materials, and no analytics to understand community engagement.

HLLC serves hundreds of local residents each week — they needed a digital foundation as strong as their community work.

🖥️Add "before" website screenshot here
What I Did

A complete digital ecosystem rebuild

  • Rebuilt the website on WordPress for a modern, accessible experience easy for the team to manage
  • Implemented Social Planet CRM to centralise contacts, communications and reporting
  • Developed a social media strategy to increase community engagement and event attendance
  • Created brand guidelines and Canva templates for consistent, on-brand communications
  • Set up Google Analytics and conversion tracking to measure impact and inform decisions

A fully integrated digital ecosystem
