Case Study 5

Building a donor engagement program from the ground up

Designing and implementing a digital fundraising and donor communications strategy to grow community support and increase giving for Pilar Indonesia.

International Development Fundraising Donor Engagement

Services: Donor communications · Email fundraising · Audience segmentation · CRM strategy

🌏 Add project mockup or visual here
The Challenge

No structured donor management or communications

There was no structured donor management or segmentation. Communications were inconsistent and generic, and donor retention was low. Pilar Indonesia needed a foundation that could grow with them.

📊 Add challenge overview visual here
What I Did

A donor engagement foundation built to last

  • Implemented a CRM from the ground up with segmentation based on engagement and giving behaviour
  • Created automated email journeys for welcome, stewardship and re-engagement
  • Developed donor appeals and impact reports aligned to campaigns
  • Built dashboards and reports to track giving, retention and engagement
  • Trained the team on ongoing use and optimisation

The Result

A strong foundation for sustainable fundraising

  • Centralised donor data and streamlined workflows
  • Automated email program increased engagement and improved retention
  • More targeted appeals leading to stronger response rates
  • Clear reporting on donor behaviour and campaign performance
  • Team empowered to manage and grow the program independently
